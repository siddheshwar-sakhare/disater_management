Wildfire Precautions

Evacuate Quickly

If you are in a wildfire-prone area, evacuate immediately when instructed by authorities. Do not wait for the fire to reach your location.

Create a Fire-Resistant Zone

Create defensible space by clearing flammable materials from around your home, like dry leaves and branches, to prevent fires from spreading.

Prepare an Emergency Kit

Keep a kit with essential supplies like water, non-perishable food, flashlights, a first-aid kit, and N95 masks in case of evacuation or poor air quality.

Stay Informed

Always stay updated with the latest news on wildfire situations through emergency alerts, social media, or news outlets.
